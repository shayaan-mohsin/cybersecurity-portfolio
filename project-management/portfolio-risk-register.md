# Portfolio Risk Register

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| Portfolio sounds too academic | Low | High | Include executive summaries, PM artifacts, public evidence, practical recommendations, and final review notes. |
| Projects overclaim enterprise experience | Low | High | Use public datasets, official frameworks, clear assumptions, and careful boundary statements. |
| Scope gets too broad | Medium | Medium | Keep each project to one clear use case and one main deliverable set. |
| Public content exposes course-restricted material | Low | High | Keep work original and based on public sources rather than copied assignments. |
| Technical artifacts are thin | Low | Medium | Include public datasets, generated outputs, a Python workflow, structured analysis tables, and ATT&CK Navigator artifact. |
| Project management evidence is hidden | Low | Medium | Maintain GitHub Project, issue history, backlog, sprint plan, roadmap, walkthrough, and final review documentation. |
