# Threat Profile

## Threat Theme

Credential theft and unauthorized access through phishing, weak authentication, and misuse of trusted business tools.

## Why It Matters

Credential-focused attacks can create downstream risk across email, cloud applications, customer data, and internal operations. For organizations with limited security maturity, these attacks are often high-impact because they exploit gaps in process, monitoring, and user awareness.

## Likely Targets

- Employee email accounts
- SaaS applications
- Shared drives
- Administrative portals
- Vendor or third-party access

## Potential Business Impact

- Account takeover
- Data exposure
- Invoice or payment fraud
- Operational disruption
- Loss of customer or stakeholder trust

## Intelligence Requirements

- What user groups are most exposed to phishing?
- Which systems lack MFA?
- Are suspicious logins reviewed?
- Are users trained to report phishing?
- Are third-party accounts monitored or reviewed?
