# Prioritized Roadmap

## 30 Days: Establish Ownership And Visibility

| Action | Owner | Risk addressed | Success measure |
|---|---|---|---|
| Assign owners for access management, vendor access, incident response, cloud storage, and asset inventory | Leadership | R-001, R-002, R-003, R-005, R-007 | Named owner documented for each area |
| Build a lightweight asset and SaaS inventory | IT Support / Office Manager | R-005 | Inventory includes owner, data type, criticality, and vendor contact |
| Validate MFA coverage for email, scheduling, billing, vendor, and privileged accounts | IT Support | R-004 | MFA status documented and exceptions tracked |
| Draft phishing reporting instructions | Office Manager / IT Support | R-006 | Staff have a clear reporting path |

## 60 Days: Formalize Access And Response

| Action | Owner | Risk addressed | Success measure |
|---|---|---|---|
| Complete first quarterly employee access review | IT Support / Operations Manager | R-001 | Stale accounts removed or justified |
| Complete first vendor access review | IT Support / Vendor Owner | R-003 | Vendor accounts mapped to business owners |
| Create incident response playbook and escalation matrix | Operations Manager / IT Support | R-002, R-009 | Playbook approved and stored centrally |
| Review cloud folder sharing permissions | Office Manager | R-007 | High-risk sharing links removed or approved |

## 90 Days: Test And Improve

| Action | Owner | Risk addressed | Success measure |
|---|---|---|---|
| Run a credential compromise tabletop exercise | Leadership / IT Support | R-002, R-009 | Lessons learned documented |
| Test recovery for one critical workflow | IT Support | R-008 | Restore process tested and gaps recorded |
| Publish annual security awareness guidance | Office Manager / Leadership | R-006 | Staff receive baseline guidance |
| Define alert review cadence for email and SaaS login activity | IT Support | R-009 | Review cadence and escalation criteria documented |

## Now / Next / Later Summary

| Timing | Focus |
|---|---|
| Now | Owners, inventory, MFA validation, phishing reporting |
| Next | Access reviews, vendor review, incident response playbook |
| Later | Tabletop exercise, recovery test, awareness program, alert review cadence |

## Success Measures

- Access reviews completed each quarter
- Asset inventory created and maintained
- Vendor account list reviewed and assigned to owners
- Incident response playbook approved
- Tabletop exercise completed
- MFA exceptions documented and reduced
- Critical workflow recovery tested
